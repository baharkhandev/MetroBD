# Metro BD Privacy Policy

Metro BD ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application processes information while providing metro card reading and metro travel utilities.

By using Metro BD, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

# 1. Information We Collect

Metro BD is designed with privacy in mind.

The application **does not collect**, **store**, or **share** personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

- Name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Home address
- Government ID
- Passwords
- Contacts
- Photos
- Device location

No account registration is required to use the application.

---

# 2. NFC Card Reading

Metro BD uses your Android device's NFC functionality to read supported Dhaka Metro cards.

Important:

- Card information is read **locally** on your device.
- Card balance and transaction history are processed entirely on your phone.
- Card information is **never transmitted** to our servers.
- We do not maintain any remote database of card information.

---

# 3. Local Storage

To improve user experience, the application may save the following data locally on your device:

- Previously scanned metro card information
- Recent transaction history
- User preferences
- Selected language
- Theme settings

This information remains only on your device and can be removed by clearing the application's data or uninstalling the application.

---

# 4. Internet Access

Metro BD may use an internet connection for:

- Checking application updates
- Loading online resources
- Opening external links
- Accessing official metro-related information (when available)

The application does not upload your NFC card information.

---

# 5. Analytics

Metro BD does not collect personal analytics.

If future versions include analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics), this Privacy Policy will be updated before those services are enabled.

---

# 6. Third-Party Services

Metro BD may provide links to third-party websites, including:

- Google Play
- Developer website
- Email applications
- Social media pages

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

---

# 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Because all NFC card processing occurs locally on your device, your metro card data remains under your control.

---

# 8. Children's Privacy

Metro BD does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Metro names, station names, and fare information are used solely for informational purposes.
